# Precision Loss

## Summary
Division before multiply in D3VaultFunding.sol

## Vulnerability Detail
Division before multiply in solidity can lead to unnecessary precision loss, which can in turn lead to loss of user funds. four such instances in D3VaultFunding.sol


## Impact
May lead to loss of user funds
